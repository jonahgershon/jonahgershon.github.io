Yield: 2 servings

1 1⁄2 lbs. boneless skinless chicken thighs or breasts
1⁄2 lemon, juiced
1⁄4 cup olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
2 tsps. ground cumin
2 tsps. paprika
1 tsp. ground tumeric
1⁄2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1⁄2 tsp. ground coriander
1⁄2 tsp. allspice (optional)
  1. Place the chicken into a plastic bag or container. Combine the rest of the ingredients and stir together. Pour the marinade over the chicken and let sit for at least 30 minutes, or overnight.

Pan Seared Shawarma:

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet (or frying pan) over high heat. Add in a few tablespoons of olive oil to the pan. Once the oil begins to shimmer and is heated, place the chicken thighs in a single layer in the pan (may have to do two batches). Sear the chicken on each side for about 2-3 minutes until golden brown, does not have to be fully cooked through yet. Remove the chicken from the pan and let rest on a cutting board for 5 minutes (leave the pan on the stove).
  2. Slice the chicken into ¼-½ inch strips. Heat the pan over high heat again with a few tablespoons of oil and place the slices of chicken back into the pan for 3-4 minutes until the slices are cooked through and crispy on the edges.

Grilled Shawarma:

  1. Preheat the grill to around 400-450℉ Spray or brush the grill grates with a thin layer of oil to prevent any sticking. Grill the chicken on high heat for around 10-15 minutes or until cooked through, making sure to flip the chicken at least once for even charring.
